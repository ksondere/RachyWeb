About Me

Hi, my name is Rachel Koller and I would love to teach you piano or voice lessons! I've been writing and performing in music since Junior High and have taught piano lessons since High School. Having graduated in 2010 with Honors from Brigham Young University's extremely selective and competitive Media Music program I now perform with the band Mayday RED, write music, and teach piano and voice lessons. I love music and want to share that love of music with you through online lessons. Feel free to contact me and let's get started.

  • Performer in the band Mayday RED
  • Producer, Writer, Performer for the Album: Step Outside
  • Have taught music lesson's extensively since graduating in 2010
